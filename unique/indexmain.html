Unique blossom schools

A Citadel of Excellence, where we nurture impeccable and selfless leaders who would impact their society positively. We are a co-educational institution located in a serene environment, in Maitama, with well-equipped and spacious classrooms that are fully air-conditioned and under a 24-hour security surveillance. We focus on harnessing the innate potentials that lie within every pupil and student placed in our care.

Proprietress Speech EDUCATION IS THE MOST POWERFUL WEAPON

Welcome back to a new session our unique Einsteins. This session, we will be promoting our pupils’ excellence through our robust curriculum, which is a blend of the Nigerian and British curricula (EYFS). In addition, the pupils will be equipped with the necessary soft skills required to navigate and impact the society positively therefore making them wholesome individuals. This session like always, we will ensure that the pupils have the opportunity to maximize individual progress towards the early learning goals and at the same time have fun while at it. Thus the 2017/2018 session is packed with fun and exciting learning activities using the standard EYFS teaching methods for best practice and activities across the prime areas of learning, personal, social and emotional development. This session will birth the ‘Let’s Explore….Series’ which will commence during our early years week. This series will enable our pupils take a voyage of imagination with appealing book reading/storytelling and colour splash, which will give the pupils a world of opportunities for experimentation and expression through. This would also include Prints making, Painting, 3-Dimensional art using beads/straws, play dough and paper crafts, music and dance, rhymes etc. On a final note, I employ every one of us to join in and explore the world through the eyes of our little ones. .
